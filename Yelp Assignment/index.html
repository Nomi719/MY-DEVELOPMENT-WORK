K-Elements BBQ

I didn't think there was anything special or unique about K-Elements. When you're comparing Korean BBQ restaurants around the bay area, this place doesn't really stand out to me. The cuts of meat aren't extraordinary and I found that many of them are marinated too salty and too fatty. There are some cuts that were so bad, we couldn't even chew through it.
